Privacy Policy

What we collect

We log your IP address (for rate limiting), the generation type selected, and the duration of each request. We do not store your input text or generated output beyond 30 days, after which logs are purged. We do not collect names, emails, or account information on the free web app.

How we use it

Usage data is used solely to operate the service, enforce rate limits, and improve generation quality. We do not sell data to third parties.

Third parties

Your input text is sent to Anthropic's API to generate output. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to that processing. We use Railway for hosting. No other third-party data processors are used.

Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold. Contact hello@networklogic.uk. As we collect minimal data by design, most requests will be fulfilled by confirming no personal data is retained.

Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. No analytics scripts are loaded on this site.
